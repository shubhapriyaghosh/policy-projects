# Strategy Memo: Constituency Priority Allocation

## Key judgment
Ward C and Ward A should be treated as the first-priority zones because they combine high local-issue intensity, anti-incumbency risk, negative online sentiment and narrow past margins.

## Recommended campaign actions

1. **Ward C - Immediate field correction**
   - Conduct issue-specific meetings on youth jobs, roads and water.
   - Deploy candidate listening tour.
   - Strengthen booth-worker coordination.

2. **Ward A - Narrative control and service guarantee**
   - Launch water and employment-focused communication.
   - Use WhatsApp micro-groups and local influencers.
   - Create a 30-day local grievance response cell.

3. **Ward E - Medium priority persuasion zone**
   - Target persuadable households with road and public-service messaging.
   - Track sentiment weekly.

## Interview talking point
This project shows how political consulting teams can combine structured data, field inputs and issue mapping to prioritise scarce campaign resources.
