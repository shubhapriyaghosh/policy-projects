# Political Risk Response Memo

## Key judgment
The campaign should prioritise negative media narratives, local protest frequency and opposition alliance strength as first-tier risks.

## Risk response playbook

1. **Narrative risk**
   - Daily media scan.
   - Rapid response fact sheet.
   - Positive local development stories.

2. **Protest risk**
   - Map grievance origin.
   - Conduct small-group listening meetings.
   - Escalate solvable issues within 7 days.

3. **Alliance risk**
   - Track opposition coordination.
   - Identify vulnerable voter blocs.
   - Increase targeted outreach in swing wards.
