# Target Operating Model: Public Service Delivery

## Operating model layers

1. **Citizen interface** - application submission, grievance, status update.
2. **Service operations** - verification, routing, approval, closure.
3. **Control tower** - SLA monitoring, backlog tracking, escalation alerts.
4. **Governance layer** - weekly review, policy decisions, accountability.

## Design principles
- One owner for every case.
- SLA breach alerts before delay becomes severe.
- Citizen communication at every major stage.
- Digital audit trail for accountability.
- Data-driven monthly policy review.
