# Scheme Evaluation Memo

## Key judgment
Schemes should not be judged only on budget spent. Reach, citizen awareness, outcome score, leakage risk and cost efficiency should determine scale decisions.

## Recommendation logic
- Scale schemes with high outcome and reach.
- Redesign schemes with poor awareness or high leakage risk.
- Pause schemes with low outcome and high cost until diagnosis is completed.

## Interview talking point
This project demonstrates monitoring and evaluation thinking: input, output, outcome and implementation quality are separated before recommendations are made.
