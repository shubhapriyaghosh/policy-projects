# OSINT Source Reliability and Confidence Rubric

## High confidence
- Official or reputed source.
- Corroborated by at least two independent sources.
- Timely and specific.
- Low bias risk.

## Medium confidence
- Plausible but partially corroborated.
- May be used with caveats.
- Should not alone support a key judgment.

## Low confidence
- Social media claim or unverified local report.
- High bias or low corroboration.
- Use only as a lead for further verification.
