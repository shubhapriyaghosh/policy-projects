# Global Security Event Monitoring Framework

## Event taxonomy
- Civil unrest
- Cross-border tension
- Shipping disruption
- Sanctions risk
- Policy uncertainty
- Terror/security incident
- Energy supply disruption

## Scoring dimensions
- Severity
- Probability
- Source reliability
- Business impact
- Escalation signal

## Analyst workflow
1. Capture event.
2. Verify with multiple source types.
3. Assign risk score.
4. Identify affected stakeholders.
5. Update executive brief.
6. Track escalation indicators.
