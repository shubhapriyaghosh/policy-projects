# Narrative Strategy Memo

## Core message
The campaign should not use a single generic development message. It should use segment-specific promises supported by visible local proof.

## Strategic recommendation
- Youth: jobs, skills and digital livelihood.
- Women: safety, water, health and daily-service reliability.
- Traders: local business ease and market infrastructure.
- Farmers: irrigation, input cost and procurement support.

## Why this matters
Segmented narrative improves persuasion because it connects the candidate promise with voter-specific pain points.
