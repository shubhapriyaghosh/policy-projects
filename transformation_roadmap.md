# AI Transformation Roadmap for Public Grievance Operations

## Executive summary
The best first-wave AI use cases are grievance triage, document verification and automated status communication because they combine high volume, high citizen impact and feasible implementation.

## Use-case waves

### Wave 1
- LLM-assisted grievance triage
- OCR-based document verification
- Automated citizen status bot

### Wave 2
- Escalation prediction model
- Department workload balancing
- Root-cause analytics

## Governance safeguards
- Human approval for sensitive cases
- Audit logs
- Escalation appeal mechanism
- Model accuracy review
- Data privacy controls

## Expected impact
- Reduced manual triage time
- Faster citizen response
- Lower repeat grievances
- Higher SLA compliance
