# Cost-to-Serve Reduction Memo

## Key judgment
Zones with high cost, high turnaround time, high backlog and high rework should be prioritised for redesign.

## Levers
- Standardised verification checklist
- First-touch automation
- Backlog war room
- Rework root-cause review
- Workflow ownership clarity

## Expected benefits
- Lower cost per case
- Lower average turnaround time
- Reduced backlog
- Improved citizen experience
